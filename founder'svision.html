Our Commitment

Every cup connects
to the soul of the mountains.

At Above 2000m Tea, we take pride in crafting premium, high-altitude teas rooted in tradition, purity, and passion. As a dedicated tea grower from the pristine hills of Ilam, I personally oversee every step from handpicking tender leaves in the cool mountain air to carefully processing them using time-honored methods.

Our elevation, rich soil, and natural environment give each batch a distinctive aroma and refined taste that reflects the true essence of Himalayan tea. We are committed not only to quality but also to preserving our heritage, supporting local communities, and delivering an authentic tea experience that connects every cup to the soul of the mountains.
